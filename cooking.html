cooking

Cooking is my creative outlet and stress relief. I love experimenting with different cuisines and techniques.

  • Currently obsessed with: Indian street food and Italian pasta
  • Favorite tools: Cast iron skillet, good knife, and a mortar & pestle
  • Go-to weeknight meal: One-pot pasta with whatever vegetables are in season
  • Weekend project: Homemade bread and fermentation experiments

I believe cooking should be fun, not stressful. Start with simple recipes, taste as you go, and don't be afraid to improvise.
